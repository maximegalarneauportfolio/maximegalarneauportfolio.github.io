Small_Doses is an open world mission designed for a 360Â° approach developped at the Campus ADN with SketchUp. This project was created in collaboration where I designed the city and the surroundings of the building.

In this hypothetical Watch_Dogs's game, the players must infiltrate the RenSense building located in UCLA Medical District of LA. They must find proof that the new nanobot composed vaccine, teased in Watch_Dogs 2's DLC Human Conditions, is not what it seems. What they are about to find out is a much more sinister plot that's going to change the world and what the players can hack. 

Design Intentions

Living City

Using modern tools like Google Earth, the UCLA Medical District of LA was recreated with the intent of being a credible location for a tech company such as RenSense to work on a nanotechnologic vaccine.

Without being a faithful recreation at the scale of the real world, some roads and minor buildings were cut. This was done to ensure that all major landmarks of the UCLA and RenSense Building are at the center of the players' attention. 

The mission is intended to be near the beginning of the game, all the mechanics are quite similars to previous Watch_Dogs' to introduce them to new players. 

Landmark

The RenSense Building is in the center of 4 main boulevards to ensure the players will see it from different angles in the city. The real UCLA Medical District has many pale buildings. In order to make sure the RenSense Building contrasts with its environment, a darker tone was chosen to help the players further notice this big landmark. 

360Â° approach

The exterior of the building has many assets to create multiple opportunities for players to experience the mission as they see fit.

Main Entrance

The players can enter the building by the Main Entrance. Once inside, it is quite difficult to avoid guards but not impossible. It is a good option for guns blazing players who like action or for ones looking for a risky stealth challenge.

Parking
Lot

The Parking Lot offers great cover with cars that players can hack to park them in different ways which could help them avoid detection. A forklift and trucks can be used to climb on top of the sheltered walkway to access the 2nd Floor through the windows. This option is great for explorers since it's not the most obvious path.

Docking
Bay

The players can take the less obvious choice and go in through the backdoor. There are more mobile guards but the players have more cover and hackable distractions to infiltrate the building without being seen. They can choose to hack a garage door to get inside or move the forklift to get to the 2nd Floor via the windows. 

Construction Crane 

Once the mission is over inside the RenSense Building, the players can escape by hacking the crane in the Construction Site accross the street. If they hack it before to get to the top floor, they won't be able to access the Director's Office inside. This is because they need 2 keys located on the 1st and 2nd Floors respectively.
