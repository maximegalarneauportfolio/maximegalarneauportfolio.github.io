Welcome Home

Created in Unity

 

Constraints

- Created a level following a rational level design document
- Introduced new ingredients and mechanics use for the rest of the game

 

Goals

- Create the third level of the game  in collaboration with a Level Artist
- Adjust the difficulty to ensure a adequate challenge progression

 

Walkthrought Duration

Approximately 12m

 

Production Time


15 weeks

PreviousNextClose



Hive is the result of a talented team of students Animators, 3D Artists, Level Designers and Programmers made in only 15 weeks while working from home. ‘’Welcome Home’’ is the third level of the game developed at the Campus ADN with Unity.

The player explores the Station of Talium Industries for the first time after being in the mines of the planet. Scared, the avatar discovers with horror the destruction of his home now invaded by the native and dangerous wildlife. Encouraged by the AI of the Station, Alexei, the avatar, must reach the Greenhouse as soon as he can, but the path is full of hazardous environments and enemies to vanquish.

The game is only in French for now.  

Design Intentions

Teach & Practice

I didn't want a fully linear path for my level design, that’s why the central room of my level had to have a big landmark. This is to ensure players recognize where they are each time they come back into it. The landmark is framed perfectly from all the entrances the players come in from.

Landmark

I didn't want a fully linear path for my level design, that’s why the central room of my level had to have a big landmark. This is to ensure players recognize where they are each time they come back into it. The landmark is framed perfectly from all the entrances the players come in from.

Rewarding Exploration

The level design included a lot of small spaces to create the impression of an inhabited station. This feeling is greatly increased by the Level Artist’s work. I used those areas to hide collectibles in order to reward the explorer and completionist players.

Rythm

This game has two main gameplay elements which are shooting and platforming. Great care was taken to leave some distance between these sections of combat and jumping to let the players breathe. I didn’t want to overburden the players with too many challenges for it is only the third level. But sometimes the fights can be quite stressfull in close area.

During this production, I also had the privilege to act as the Lead Game Designer and as a Progammer. 

My main tasks was to design, with a team, the mecanics and ensure they are balanced during all the game.  I also had to check and approved many assets to ensure their cohesion.

I helped as support programmer for smaller tasks to help the script's team as much as I could.
