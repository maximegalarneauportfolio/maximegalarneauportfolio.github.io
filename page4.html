Hunter's Manor

Created in UNREAL 4

 

Constraints

- Cannot use any words or dialogue
- Only use free assets
- Must have outdoor and indoor environments 

 

Goals

Make the player feel the vastness and the credibility of a big modern Manor 

 

Walkthrough Duration

Approximately 2m30s

 

Production Time

42 hours

PreviousNextClose



Hunter's Manor is a narrative level developped at the Campus ADN with while learning how to use Unreal 4.

The player explores a Hunter's Manor which is more a memorial of his past hunts. While exploring, the player will discover that the owner of this mansion does not only hunt normal creatures but mythical ones as well. 

There's an evolution with the taxidermied beasts in addition to the paintings on display in order to raise the tension until the last revelation.

Design Intentions

Narrative Environment

Without any words or dialogue, part of this exercise was to let the players discover and create the story by themself while using the assets as a environmental design story. There's an evolution with the taxidermied beasts in addition to the paintings on display in order to raise the disbelief of the players about this strange manor.

Guidance

The light was used to attract the players into the right direction. It was always possible for them to explore the manor as they wished.
The fireplace, with it moving lights, is a good way to attract the attention of the players once they enter the mansion in order to make them understand what kind of place it is.

Rewarding Curiosity

Once the players find the biggest room with all the mythical creatures exposed, their curiosity can lead them to a more linear path in a ''S'' form. Each corner reveal a bigger and more menacing creatures until the final revelation of a dragon.
