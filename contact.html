About me

I am Maxime Galarneau and after many years in sales, I decided I had to reorient my career towards a domain that both inspires me and that I am passionate about.

‘’Why video games?’’ The real question should be ''why NOT video games?''. It is a media form that includes all others : visual art, literature, cinema, music and many more, all within an active experience rather than a passive one.  

My goal as a Level Designer is to bring those enriching and compelling experiences to life for players to enjoy. It is of the utmost importance for me to do whatever it takes to bring a project to release. Teamwork is essential to achieve this objective, this is why I highly prioritize my interpersonal skills as well as supporting my colleagues in an effort to rally and inspire groups towards successful results.
